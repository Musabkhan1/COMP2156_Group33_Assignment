DevOps plays a crucial role in the Software Development Lifecycle (SDLC) by bridging the gap between development and operations. 
It helps improve efficiency, reduce deployment risks, and enhance collaboration among teams.

The key benefits of DevOps in SDLC include:
- **Faster Development Cycles:** Continuous integration and automated testing speed up software releases.
- **Improved Collaboration:** Developers and IT operations teams work together seamlessly.
- **Better Reliability:** Continuous monitoring ensures system stability and quick issue resolution.
- **Automation:** Reduces manual errors and increases deployment efficiency.
- **Scalability:** Cloud-based DevOps tools allow businesses to scale their applications easily.

By adopting DevOps practices, organizations can deliver software faster, improve quality, and enhance customer satisfaction.
